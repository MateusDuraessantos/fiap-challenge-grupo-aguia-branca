## 📖 About The Project

**INOVAGAB** is a corporate innovation management platform developed for the **FIAP + Grupo Águia Branca Innovation Challenge**.

The project was designed to connect **strategy**, **people**, **processes**, and **technology** into a single ecosystem capable of transforming operational problems and employee ideas into measurable business results.

The platform focuses on increasing innovation visibility, employee engagement, and project execution efficiency across large organizations.

---

## 🎯 Main Goals

- Capture operational problems and innovation opportunities
- Transform ideas into structured initiatives
- Connect innovation with business strategy
- Monitor project execution and performance
- Measure ROI and innovation impact
- Encourage continuous employee participation
